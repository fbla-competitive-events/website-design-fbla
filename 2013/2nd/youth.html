Redmond's Great Day of Play


Redmond City Hall Campus

15670 NE 85th St.
Redmond, WA 98052
(425) 556-2900

August 24, 2013, 3-7pm

This year Redmond will be hosting their annual Great Day of Play on August 24th from 3pm-7pm. All ages are welcome; the event will take place at the Redmond City Hall Campus. Families that value health, fitness and active recreation will enjoy this event. Take part in the national movement to make America more active. Activities such as Kangoo Jumps, Spin Biking, Z-Ultimate martial arts, and Jazzercise are offered and taught. Additionally, a 3-on-3 youth basketball tournament will be held from 2pm-7pm. Kids ages 4-12 are encouraged to drop by and participate. During the same time, a 5K run/walk will be held on the Sammamish River Trail. Don't miss out on this opportunity to get up and play!

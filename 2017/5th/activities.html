Go-Karts

Come race your friends at our go-kart race track. With 16 cars available for every race, you'll be sure to find some competition. Every race is 3 laps to give each contestant a fair chance to win. If you are in the top 3, then you get a lap free of charge!

Laser Tag

Our laser tag arena spans over 500 square feet which gives you a lot of places to hide and ambush your friends. Most games are 5 vs. 5, but we have the gear to go up to a 10 vs. 10 if you have a large party. Just remember to make a reservation if you have a large party so we can make sure that everything is ready.

Mini Golf

If you are looking to just spend some relaxing time with your family, then mini golf is the way to go. We offer an 18 hole course complete with windmills and dinosaurs. We also made sure to include different size putters to allow everyone the chance to play.

Arcade

Whether it's to race your friends, shoot zombies or enjoy some of the classics, we have everything you could need in our one-of-a-kind arcade. Our arcade is loaded with award-winning games and an amazing atmosphere. All of this is located in our state-of-the-art facility, so come on over and see for yourself just why everyone has been talking about FunWorks Warehouse.

Buffet

From delicious lasagna to boneless wings to chocolate cake, our buffet has everything you need to be fueled and ready for all the fun you will have at FunWorks Warehouse. We have an excellent staff trained to cook what you want the way you want it. So head on over to FunWorks Warehouse to fuel up for your entertainment adventure!

Child Play Center

Even if you have small children we still have a place for them to play and have tons of fun. From jungle gyms to ball pits, it's sure to be a blast. We also have cubbies with optional combination locks to keep your child's small items safe while they are playing.

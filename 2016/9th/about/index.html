About Us

History

The idea started when we realized how important healthy food should be for everyone. As a team of professional bakers and chefs, we pride ourselves in the food that we create; it is only reasonable that we should consider "good" in terms of health as well! We do not simply bake for taste. We bake for a cause. And that cause is your health. That is why we decided to open Bready, a restauarant and bakery where you can come in and enjoy gluten-free foods!
